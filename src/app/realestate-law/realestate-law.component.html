Conveyancing

Working on commercial or residential cases on behalf of individuals, companies, developers or public bodies. We can act for you in all types of conveyancing transactions, including freehold, leasehold, new build and shared ownership. Conveyancing is a legal term used to describe the process of transferring ownership of property from a seller to a buyer or vice versa.

Please find below a list of services we offer:

  • Equity release
  • Deed of Variation
  • Leasehold purchases.
  • Purchasing freehold property
  • Right to buy
  • Re-mortgages
  • Selling property
  • Shared ownership.
