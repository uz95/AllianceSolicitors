Alliance Solicitors have been helping clients for several years with various legal matters ranging from complex, such as immigration, family, criminal and civil law to everyday matters ranging from wills & probate, employment law & conveyancing.

Our approach in most matters is simple. You visit or call us, a solicitor will assess your matter, the solicitor would draft legal documents where and when necessary and if appropriate we will instruct a barrister who will provide legal advice and appear in court.

Meet Our Team

At Alliance Solicitors we have a dedicated team of solicitors and barristers with over 40 years of collective experience who offer a wide range of legal services to individuals and businesses across all sectors. Our Solicitors are highly qualified individuals who are on hand to take your calls and answer any questions you may have with legal matters.

We have several members of the team who may work on your matter. Regardless of who works on your matter, they will be supervised by Nazia Munir, Principal Solicitor and Head of Family, Civil and Immigration.

Muhammad Tariq - Principal Solicitor and Supervisor

Tariq is the head of our team and has significant experience in private client work, specializing in family, civil and immigration law. Tariq has been working in these areas since she qualified and has helped several families and individuals with successful outcomes.

Babar Khan - Practice Manager

Babar has successfully completed his LLB. LPC & LLM. Babar has experience in private immigration matters. leasehold matter and employment law. Babar has successfully helped clients with further leave to remain applications, entry clearance applications, asylum matters. EU applications and also judicial reviews.

Babar also assists with business leasehold matters and has extensive experience in this area of law

Babar is fluent in the following languages: Urdu, English, Punjabi, Portuguese & Spanish

Snezhana Ana Postolovska- Consultant Solicitor

Ana specialises in all aspects of business & high-net worth immigration matters. Ana's extensive experience in advising high-net worth clients has been extremely successful; Ana explores the most suitable options available for clients and then advises accordingly.

Ana specialises in advising individuals and businesses with all Points Based System (PBS) applications which include Start-up, Innovator, Global Talent. Tier 1 Entrepreneur and Tier 1 Investor Visa. Ana also has experience in Sole Representative Visa, Settlement, Nationality and European immigration matters.

Ana has assisted companies with obtaining sponsor licences, work permits for employees. advises them on their duties and obligations as sponsors, right to work checks and conducts mock audits.

Ana is fluent in the following languages: Bosnian, Bulgarian, Croatian. English, Macedonian & Serbian.
