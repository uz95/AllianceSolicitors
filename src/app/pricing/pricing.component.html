Our Fees Explained

Understanding how your legal costs are calculated is a subject we take seriously and this is why we only have 3 options available to ensure complete transparency from the outset. We will make sure you have all the information you need from our initial consultation to make an informed choice.s

Option 1- Fixed Fee

At Alliance Solicitors the option of a fixed fee is available on most of our matters. You will find below a list of matters with fixed fees. Please note if your matter is not straight forward we will need to discuss your case in full to understand your instructions and once this has been clarified we will then be in a position to offer you a fixed fee or an alternative.

Option 2 - Hourly Charge

This is our standard fee structure. This option allows you to appoint a solicitor on an hour by hour basis. The amount you pay for the hour will be based on the experience of the solicitor you would like to appoint. We will discuss the matter with you and will then be in a position to quote you for your matter. Hourly rates at Alliance Solicitors range from £150-£300.

Option 3-Conditional or Contingent Fee Arrangement

This option is only available once we have assessed the case in full. We will discuss the case with you on a free one hour consultation. We will then spend 2 hours reviewing and assessing any evidence supplied. There will be no cost involved if we decline the offer to accept your matter we will agree a fee upfront. This fee is normally a percentage paid to Alliance Solicitors from any damages awarded.
