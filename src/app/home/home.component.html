The Process we Follow

Appointing the right solicitor is crucial for a successful outcome of your matter. Solicitors have areas of expertise and this is why it's always important for you to do your own research and instruct a solicitor who has competence in achieving your desired result.

At Alliance Solicitors we listen and understand your legal problems first. Call us now for a free no obligation consultation on +44 (0) 20 8774 9481

Instructing A Solicitor

Do your homework and make sure you both understand each other. this is crucial & helps save costs.

Giving Your Instructions

Have a brief ready, write bullet points, what problem do you have and what help do you need.

Keep It Smile

Avoid emotions and keep things factual when presenting your argument. Law is evidence based.

Costs

Make sure costs are always agreed up front. No hidden surprises. Always challenge discrepancies.

Access To Solicitors

Make sure you have access to your Solicitors, remotely or in person. Alliance Solicitors can do both.

Ask Questions

Don't be afraid to ask questions. You need to know how your case is progressing at all times.
